About HorseShowCalendar.com

Your comprehensive guide to horse shows across the United States.

Our Mission

HorseShowCalendar.com was created to solve a simple problem: finding horse shows shouldn't be hard. Whether you compete in dressage, hunter/jumper, eventing, western, reining, barrel racing, breed shows, polo, driving, or combined tests, we believe every equestrian deserves a single, reliable place to find upcoming competitions.

What We Do

We aggregate horse show schedules from across the country, organizing them by discipline, region, and date so you can quickly find the events that matter to you. Our listings include essential details like entry fees, stabling availability, prize money, judges, and sanctioning organizations.

We cover shows at every level:

  • Schooling shows for green horses and developing riders
  • Recognized competitions sanctioned by USEF, AQHA, USDF, USEA, NRHA, WPRA, AHA, USPA, and ADS
  • Championship events including regional and national finals

For Show Organizers

If you organize horse shows, we invite you to list your events on our calendar at no cost. Simply use the Submit a Show form to provide your show details. Our team reviews all submissions and typically publishes listings within 2-3 business days.
