Frequently Asked Questions

Use the "Submit a Show" form above to send us your show information. Include the show name, dates, location, discipline, and your contact email. Our team reviews submissions within 2-3 business days and will add your show to the calendar. Listing is completely free for show organizers.

HorseShowCalendar.com is an independent aggregator and is not officially affiliated with USEF, AQHA, USDF, USEA, NRHA, or any other governing body. We compile show information from public sources and organizer submissions to provide a comprehensive calendar. Always verify details directly with the show organizer or sanctioning body.

We update the calendar weekly with new shows and any changes to existing listings. Show organizers can submit updates at any time through our submission form. If you notice an error or a show that has been cancelled, please contact us so we can update the listing promptly.

HorseShowCalendar.com is a directory and does not process show entries. Each show listing includes a link to the show's official website where you can find entry forms, prize lists, and registration information. We recommend contacting the show secretary directly for entry questions.

Our weekly alert email includes a curated list of upcoming shows that match your selected disciplines and region. You'll see show names, dates, locations, and direct links to details. Alerts are sent every Monday morning, and you can unsubscribe or update your preferences at any time.
